Introduction

A coat of paint can change everything.

Whether it's the curb appeal of your home, the personality of a single room, or a deck that's lost its shine — we treat every project like it's the only one on the calendar.

Color Coat is a custom painting and deck restoration team built on three simple principles: prep matters, materials matter, and finish work tells the whole story.

Section 04 — Our Craft

Beautiful work is boring work, done right.

Painting looks easy until you've watched a fresh coat peel because the surface wasn't prepped properly. Or seen brushstrokes texture a wall because the wrong roller was used. Or had paint bleed under tape that wasn't laid right.

We've spent years sweating those details so you don't have to. The result is a finish that looks great the day it's done — and still looks great five years later.

Our Promise

We don't paint fast. We paint well. And when we're done, you'll see the difference in every line, edge, and corner.
